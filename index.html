What is Third Position Values?

Third Position Values is a test that attempts to guess and specify your ideology in the broad term of Third Position. You will be presented with a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

What are the values?

There are four axes; with the first two making a square compass and the remaining being independent:

STATE
How much should the state intervene in the economy?
OWNERSHIP
What should be owned by the collective and what could be private?
TRADITION
Should the future resemble the past, or become something new?
FAITH
Should the state remain secular and is theism any good?
